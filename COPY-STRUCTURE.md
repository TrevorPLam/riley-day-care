## Page & Messaging Outline

This file captures high-level messaging and CTAs for each core page.

### Home

- **Primary goal**: Get parents to schedule a tour or call.
- **Hero**:
  - Headline: Emphasize safe, nurturing daycare in Southeast Dallas.
  - Subheadline: Mention small-group attention and licensed caregivers.
  - CTAs: Primary **“Schedule a Tour”**, secondary **“Call Now”** linking to `(972) 286-0357`.
- **Key sections**:
  - Benefits overview (safety, learning, loving environment, convenient location).
  - Snapshot of daily life (short bullets and photo grid).
  - Highlight of programs/age groups.
  - Trust band with licensing, years in operation (when known), and brief testimonial.

### About

- **Goal**: Deepen trust and show who is caring for their child.
- **Content**:
  - Brief story of Riley Day Care and its mission.
  - Philosophy on early childhood education and care.
  - Staff section with names, roles, and short bios (including credentials like CPR/first aid).
  - Safety and cleanliness practices summary.
- **CTA**: Invite parents to read programs or schedule a tour.

### Programs

- **Goal**: Help parents quickly see if there is a fit for their child’s age and needs.
- **Content**:
  - Cards or sections for each age group (e.g., infants, toddlers, preschool).
  - Typical daily schedule highlights (play, meals, rest, learning activities).
  - Ratios or small-group emphasis where applicable.
  - Notes on curriculum, play-based learning, and social/emotional development.
- **CTA**: “Check availability for your child’s age” and link to Enrollment / Schedule a Tour.

### Tuition & Hours

- **Goal**: Provide clarity on cost expectations and schedule, reduce friction and surprise.
- **Content**:
  - Hours of operation and pickup/drop-off windows.
  - High-level tuition structure (e.g., full-time/part-time, weekly rates or “contact us for exact tuition but here is what’s included”).
  - What tuition includes (meals, snacks, supplies, curriculum materials).
  - Any discounts, assistance programs, or accepted subsidies (if applicable).
- **CTA**: “Request current tuition and availability” and link to Enrollment form.

### Enrollment / Schedule a Tour

- **Goal**: Capture qualified leads through a simple, reassuring form.
- **Content**:
  - Short intro explaining the process (tour, meet staff, confirm availability, complete paperwork).
  - Form fields:
    - Parent/guardian name
    - Child’s name and age
    - Preferred start date
    - Phone and email
    - Message/notes (optional)
  - Reassurance copy: privacy, no obligation, quick follow-up expectations.
- **Primary CTA**: “Schedule a Tour”.

### FAQ

- **Goal**: Answer common objections and questions to reduce hesitation.
- **Topics** (examples):
  - Safety and security procedures.
  - Meals and snacks (what’s provided, allergies).
  - Nap time routines.
  - Illness and medication policies.
  - Parent communication (daily updates, photos, messages).
  - Holiday/closure schedule expectations.
- **CTA**: If your question isn’t answered, “Contact us” or “Schedule a Tour”.

### Contact

- **Goal**: Make it extremely easy to get in touch and find the daycare.
- **Content**:
  - Name, full address, and phone:
    - Riley Day Care  
    - 1509 Haymarket Rd, Dallas, TX 75253  
    - (972) 286-0357
  - Click-to-call and mailto actions.
  - Embedded map and brief driving directions from major nearby roads/landmarks.
  - Simple contact form (can reuse Enrollment form structure for general inquiries with a different label).
- **CTA**: “Call Now” and “Send a Message”.
